Admission Procedure

Eligibility

Admissions for 6th standard will be taken through entance exam. Students studying in 5th standard in any recognised school of Karnataka state are eligible to apply.

The annual income of the family of a candidate should be below the following limit to claim reservation.
Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe : 2,50,000/-
Category-1 : 2,50,000/-
Other Backward Classes : 1,00,000/-
