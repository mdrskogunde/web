Welcome!

Morarji Desai Residential School (SC), Kogunde is spread over 10 acres in Bahaddurghatta, a quiet and picturesque village located 36 kilometres away from the historical city of Chitradurga. Set in a lush green environment, the campus presents a very peaceful ambience for a safe and positive learning environment.

Learn more

About Us

Our school is administered by Karnataka Residential Educational Institutions Society, an organisation of Govt. of Karnataka, established to provide quality education to socially backward and underprivileged classes of the society.

We provide a happy, calm and exciting place to learn, where children get a high quality, innovative and creative learning experience, delivered through excellent teaching and up to date technology.
